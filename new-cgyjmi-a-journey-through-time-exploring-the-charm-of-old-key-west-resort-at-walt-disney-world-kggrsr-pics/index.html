A Journey Through Time: Exploring the Charm of Old Key West Resort at Walt Disney World

Old Key West Resort, a Disney Vacation Club resort, offers a unique blend of laid-back island charm and Disney magic. Nestled amidst lush greenery and shimmering waterways, this resort transports guests to a nostalgic era of Key West, Florida, evoking the spirit of a bygone era with its charming architecture, vibrant colors, and relaxed atmosphere.

A Glimpse into the Past:

The resort’s design meticulously recreates the ambiance of Key West’s historic architecture, boasting pastel-hued buildings adorned with gingerbread trim, intricate wrought iron balconies, and lush tropical landscaping. The resort’s central hub, aptly named "The Settlement," serves as a lively gathering place, housing the resort’s main pool, a charming marina, restaurants, and shops.

Navigating the Resort: A Map Unveils the Treasures

Understanding the layout of Old Key West Resort is crucial for maximizing your vacation experience. The resort map, a treasure trove of information, serves as your guide to navigating this expansive property.

Unveiling the Map’s Secrets:

  • Condominiums and Villas: The map clearly outlines the various accommodation options, highlighting the unique features of each villa type. From cozy studios to spacious three-bedroom villas, guests can choose the accommodation that best suits their needs and preferences.
  • Pool and Recreation: The map pinpoints the resort’s various recreational amenities, including the main pool, "The Sandcastle Pool," with its whimsical pirate ship, and the tranquil "Quiet Pool." It also indicates the location of the fitness center, arcade, and playground.
  • Dining and Shopping: The map acts as a culinary compass, guiding guests to the resort’s diverse dining options, from casual eateries like "Olivia’s Cafe" to the upscale "Gurgling Suitcase." It also points out the location of the "Old Key West General Store," offering essential sundries and souvenirs.
  • Transportation: The map is a valuable tool for navigating the resort’s internal transportation system, including the resort’s complimentary shuttle service, which connects guests to Disney’s theme parks, water parks, and Disney Springs.

Beyond the Map: Exploring the Resort’s Hidden Gems

While the map provides a comprehensive overview, it’s worth venturing beyond its boundaries to discover the resort’s hidden gems.

  • Charming Waterways: Take a leisurely stroll along the resort’s picturesque waterways, where you might encounter friendly ducks and turtles.
  • Hidden Trails: Explore the resort’s hidden trails, winding through lush vegetation, offering tranquil moments of escape.
  • Firefly Nights: During the summer months, witness the enchanting spectacle of fireflies illuminating the resort’s night sky, a magical experience for all ages.

FAQs: Unraveling the Mysteries of Old Key West Resort

Q: What are the different types of accommodations available at Old Key West Resort?

A: Old Key West Resort offers a variety of accommodation options, including studios, one-bedroom villas, two-bedroom villas, and three-bedroom villas, each with unique features and amenities.

Q: What are the dining options at Old Key West Resort?

A: The resort offers a diverse range of dining options, from casual eateries like "Olivia’s Cafe" and "The Boatwright’s Dining Hall" to the upscale "Gurgling Suitcase."

Q: Does Old Key West Resort offer transportation to the Disney theme parks?

A: Yes, the resort provides complimentary transportation to Disney’s theme parks, water parks, and Disney Springs via a dedicated shuttle service.

Q: What are the recreational activities available at Old Key West Resort?

A: The resort offers a wide range of recreational activities, including swimming, fishing, boating, biking, and more.

Q: Is Old Key West Resort suitable for families with children?

A: Yes, Old Key West Resort is a family-friendly resort with a variety of activities and amenities designed for children, including playgrounds, a kids’ pool, and a variety of family-friendly dining options.

Tips for an Unforgettable Stay at Old Key West Resort:

  • Plan Your Transportation: Familiarize yourself with the resort’s transportation options and schedule your trips in advance, especially for theme park visits.
  • Explore the Resort’s Waterways: Take advantage of the resort’s scenic waterways by taking a leisurely stroll or renting a boat.
  • Enjoy the Resort’s Pools: Relax and unwind at the resort’s various pools, each offering a unique atmosphere.
  • Dine at the Resort’s Restaurants: Experience the resort’s diverse dining options, from casual to upscale.
  • Attend the Resort’s Activities: Participate in the resort’s daily activities, including campfire sing-alongs and movie nights.

Conclusion: A Timeless Escape

Old Key West Resort offers a unique blend of nostalgia, comfort, and Disney magic. Its charming architecture, vibrant atmosphere, and exceptional amenities create a truly unforgettable vacation experience. Whether you’re seeking a relaxing getaway or a family-friendly adventure, Old Key West Resort provides a timeless escape, transporting guests to a bygone era of Key West charm.
